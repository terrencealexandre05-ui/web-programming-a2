Our Story

Made With Love,
for Filipinas

A brand born from the belief that every shade of Filipino skin is beautiful.

Colourette believes in beauty in all ways, always.

We're all about creating products that are proudly made by Filipinos for Filipinos. We started in 2015 with our goal being to empower Filipinos and ensure that they see themselves in advertisements, social media, and campaigns.

Everyone deserves to feel represented! Our team is made up of Peaches who believe in the brand's mission and make continuous efforts to achieve it.

Colourette is certified cruelty-free by PETA's Global Beauty Without Bunnies Program because we believe that cruelty has no place in makeup. Our formulas are also vegan and paraben-free — so anyone and everyone can use it!
